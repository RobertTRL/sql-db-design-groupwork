# INSTRUCTIONS

Tomashi Logistics is a transportation company in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) with a fleet of 10 medium-sized trucks. The company wants to build a database to track the revenue and expenses of each truck.

Each truck is assigned to a driver and makes many trips. Every trip generates revenue. The company also records expenses such as fuel, maintenance, repairs, and driver allowances for each truck.

The system should allow the company to:

1. Record trucks, drivers, trips, revenues, and expenses.
2. Calculate the total revenue and total expenses for each truck.
3. Determine the profit made by each truck each month.
4. Identify the most profitable truck and the truck with the highest expenses.

Design and implement a relational database that stores this information and supports these reports.
